--- title: Get Involved ---

Help influence future recommendations by participating in our discussions. Anybody who subscribes to the Google Group is part of the PHP-FIG.

As soon as you subscribe to the mailing list and/or join the Discord Server you are a PHP-FIG Community Member, who can influence standards, make suggestions, give feedback, etc. Only PHP-FIG Voting Members can start or participate in votes, but the discussion and formation stages involve everyone.

  • Follow us @phpfig
  • Discuss on Discord
  • Contribute via GitHub
  • Join our mailing list
